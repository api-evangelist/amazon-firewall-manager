AWS announces Spring 2026 System and Organization Controls reports in machine-readable OSCAL format alongside traditional PDF versions. The reports cover 188 services over a 12-month period, making AWS the first major cloud provider offering compliance reports in NIST's Open Security Controls Assessment Language format, enabling workflow automation and reduced manual processing for security and compliance operations.
