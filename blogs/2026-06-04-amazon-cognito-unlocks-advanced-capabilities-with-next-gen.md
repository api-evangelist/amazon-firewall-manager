Amazon Cognito's infrastructure modernization enables new features including high-throughput performance for large-scale workloads, customer-managed encryption keys via AWS KMS, and multi-Region replication for business continuity. The authors share lessons learned from migrating hundreds of millions of user profiles with zero downtime, including shadow mode validation and dual-write architecture strategies.
