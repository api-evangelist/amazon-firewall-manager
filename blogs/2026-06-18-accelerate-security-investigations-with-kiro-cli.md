Kiro CLI, an AI-powered assistant, helps security teams investigate AWS incidents faster by proposing AWS CLI commands, explaining their purpose, and waiting for approval before executing them. The post demonstrates investigating a GuardDuty finding following the AWS Security Incident Response Guide framework, covering discovery, resource analysis, containment, evidence preservation, and CloudTrail analysis, letting analysts complete investigations in minutes rather than hours while automatically maintaining compliance documentation.
